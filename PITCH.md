### 🎨 Natural as Pen & Paper

Forget complex operations. Here, you just need to:
- **Press & Hold** - Start writing
- **Release** - Complete a stroke
- **Click Save** - Preserve forever

That's it. As natural as drawing on paper.

## 🌟 Key Features

### 🎨 Fluid Drawing

- ✅ Silky smooth rendering
- ✅ Pressure sensitivity
- ✅ 6 preset colors
- ✅ 5 stroke sizes

### 🛠️ Practical Tools

- ✅ Pen/Eraser toggle
- ✅ Undo/Redo
- ✅ Clear canvas
- ✅ Keyboard shortcuts

### 💾 Multi-format Export

| Format | Use Case | Scenario |
|--------|----------|----------|
| **PNG** | HD Image | Sharing, documents |
| **PDF** | Print Doc | Formal submission |
| **JSON** | Raw Data | Re-edit, backup |

### ⌨️ Productivity Shortcuts

```
Ctrl+Z    →  Undo
Ctrl+S    →  Save
Delete    →  Clear
Esc       →  Close dialog
```
