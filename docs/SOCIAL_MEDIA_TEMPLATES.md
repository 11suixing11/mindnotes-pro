## Product Hunt

### Tagline
The beautiful, privacy-first whiteboard that works offline

### Description
MindNotes Pro is a local-first whiteboard drawing app with a stunning Monet-inspired design. Unlike other whiteboard tools that require cloud accounts and servers, MindNotes Pro keeps all your data in your browser — completely private, completely offline.

**Why MindNotes Pro?**

🎨 **Beautiful Design** — Watercolor gradients, paper textures, and glass morphism effects inspired by Monet's impressionist palette

🔒 **Privacy First** — Zero cloud dependency. No accounts, no tracking, no servers. Your data stays in your browser.

⚡ **Lightning Fast** — Only 3 core dependencies. Loads in under 1 second. Works offline as a PWA.

📝 **Feature Rich** — Freehand drawing, shapes, text, multi-select, undo/redo, dark mode, PDF export, and document management.

🛠 **Developer Friendly** — TypeScript strict mode, Zustand architecture, comprehensive tests, clean codebase.
